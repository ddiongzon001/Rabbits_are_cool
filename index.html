Rabbits are cool!

Rabbits are fuzzy, cute and cuddly. Some rabbits can last up to 15 years! How exciting!

Rabbits can be very small or very big. The smallest rabbits are less than a foot long while bigger rabbits can be bigger than 4 feet! How cool!

If you ever have a rabbit for a pet, be sure to take care of it and it will love you back as well.
